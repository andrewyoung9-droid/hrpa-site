Privacy is a Human Right.

HPRA helps people facing doxxing, stalking, harassment, and surveillance—online and offline—by providing support, education, and advocacy. We also collaborate with law enforcement and train the next generation of privacy defenders.

Our Mission

We empower individuals, educate communities, and strengthen the ability of law enforcement to protect privacy in both digital and physical spaces. We believe privacy is not a luxury—it is a human right.

Why this matters

The digital landscape is under‑enforced. Many victims of cyberstalking or coordinated harassment struggle to get help or be taken seriously. HPRA fills that gap with practical support and advocacy.

MN focus, broad impact

We serve Minnesota communities while publishing resources anyone can use. Our approach complements—not duplicates—existing state and county services.
