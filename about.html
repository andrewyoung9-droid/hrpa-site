About HPRA

We are a Minnesota nonprofit dedicated to digital and personal privacy. HPRA helps people facing cyberstalking, coordinated harassment, online smear campaigns, and intrusive surveillance—and equips communities to prevent harm before it starts.

Our Mission

We defend privacy as a human right. We provide practical support for victims, publish plain‑language guides, and advocate for Minnesota‑first policies that protect people online and off.

Core Values

  • Dignity & Safety — survivors first, always
  • Clarity — plain language, practical steps
  • Privacy by Default — minimal data, strong protections
  • Community — we partner with local orgs, schools, and advocates

What We Do

Direct victim support, community education, and policy advocacy focused on Minnesota’s needs.

How We Work

Privacy‑first operations: minimal data collection, secure communications, and survivor‑led practices.

Who We Serve

Individuals, families, schools, and community orgs impacted by online harms and digital threats.
