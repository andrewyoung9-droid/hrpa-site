Get Help Now

If you’re in immediate danger, call your local emergency number. Otherwise, follow these steps to stabilize your accounts and start a safe paper trail.

View checklists Email HPRA

First steps (10–20 minutes)

Secure your accounts

Change passwords (unique + manager), enable MFA, update recovery options, and sign out of other sessions.

Capture evidence

Screenshot messages, save URLs, note dates/times. Don’t engage; document calmly.

Reduce exposure

Lock down privacy settings, review app permissions, and consider limiting public posts temporarily.

Report & Support

Report to platforms

Use built-in tools to report harassment, impersonation, and doxxing. Keep ticket numbers.

Talk to someone

Reach a trusted person or a local support line. If mental health is a concern, consider seeking professional support.

Contact HPRA

We can help you prioritize steps and organize evidence. help@humanprivacyrightsalliance.org
